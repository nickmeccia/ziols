Today, more than ever, it is important that you maintain healthy teeth and gums. Research is showing that poor oral health can be a major contributing factor to heart disease, diabetes, premature term pregnancies, chronic inflammatory diseases, and many other health conditions. We are your center for heart-healthy dentistry.
